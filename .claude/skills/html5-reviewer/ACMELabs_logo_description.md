Main Title: "A C M E L a B S"
Typeface: Acme (specifically the Bold weight). This font family is designed by Mojomox.

Characteristics: It is a modern, geometric sans-serif font. The most distinctive feature is the lowercase 'a' in "L a B S," which is not a modification but a unique character included within this specific typeface's glyph set. This deliberate choice creates a memorable mixed-case styling. The letters are all-caps except for that 'a' and are set with significant spacing (tracking).

2. Slogan Text: "Lets Create Something" and "Out of Nothing"
   Typeface: Montserrat (specifically the Regular weight).

Characteristics: This is a very popular, geometric sans-serif font known for its versatility and clean appearance. It matches the geometry of the main font perfectly, creating a cohesive brand look.

In summary, the logo achieves its professional and unique look through the use of:

Primary Font: Acme Bold (from Mojomox) with mixed-case styling and wide spacing.

Secondary Font: Montserrat Regular for the smaller supporting slogans.
