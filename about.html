About

I'm Ids Achterhof — an independent solutions architect based in the Netherlands with over 30 years of experience in enterprise architecture. I help organizations design, build, and evolve systems that are resilient, scalable, and fit for purpose.

Deep in building mode — tinkering with architecture patterns, chasing fresh ideas, and bridging the gap between business needs and technical solutions. After decades of working with enterprise systems, every new challenge still feels like a puzzle worth solving.

What I Do

My work sits at the intersection of business strategy and technical execution. I design enterprise architectures that balance complexity with maintainability, and I help teams navigate the decisions that shape their systems for years to come.

Enterprise Architecture
Designing systems that align technology with business goals — from strategy to implementation.
Solutions Architecture
Translating complex requirements into scalable, maintainable technical solutions.
Technical Leadership
Guiding teams through architectural decisions, technology selection, and migration strategies.
System Resilience
Building systems that handle the unexpected gracefully — from infrastructure failures to evolving requirements.
